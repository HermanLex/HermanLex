Donate to SB Web Kids

SB Web Kids is a registered California non-profit organization. Your generous contribution can help us provide even more children with the same resources that make SB Web Kids engaging and innovative.

Financial contributions to SB Web Kids are entirely voluntary, and not a condition of participation in the program, however volunteer efforts and financial support have been essential in our persuit of providing a premium quality curriculum, classroom, and equipment for under-represented children. We rely on parents and the community to provide for everything so that participanting children can focus more on learning and less on worrying about how to afford the premium experiences that we offer.


Have questions?

What do donations cover?

Student computers, a modern & comfortable classroom with lightning fast wifi, creative supplies, inspiring field trips to meet top tier professional web designers, healthy snack breaks and many other student support services at SB Web Kids which can only be made possible with donations from individuals and organizations.
