SKILLS FOR CREATIVE SUCCESS

About

Web Kids delivers a premium web design course (for free) to youth who may otherwise not have access. Our mission is to empower diverse kids with skills for lasting career success at tech companies.

We engage youth from diverse backgrounds, especially those who are underrepresented in current web and software design roles. Our end goal is to inspire continued learning and prepare students for creative success.

Our approach

  • A 2 to 4 week creative web design course where:
  • Students gain valuable computer literacy skills.
  • We have fun exploring creative storytelling methods.
  • Students are introduced to established career designers in top organizations.
